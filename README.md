# EstateBridge

EstateBridge is a SaaS property management platform built for UK estate 
agencies, US real estate brokerages, and landlord teams. It replaces the 
fragmented stack of portal logins, spreadsheets, and standalone CRMs with 
a single dashboard that handles everything from listing creation to lead 
conversion.

## What it does

- **Portal syndication** — Connect your own Rightmove, Zoopla, OnTheMarket, 
  Zillow, and Trulia API credentials and push listings live, update prices, 
  and remove stock when it sells or lets — all from one property hub.

- **Property CRM** — Manage enquiries, follow-ups, viewings, and notes in 
  a pipeline built for sales and lettings teams. Leads arrive with full 
  context so your team knows what each buyer wanted before the first call.

- **AI listing tools** — Draft short summaries, full descriptions, and 
  feature bullet lists with built-in AI. Import details from an existing 
  listing URL to avoid retyping. All output goes through human review 
  before publishing.

- **Branded agency website** — Every account gets a searchable, branded 
  property website with live listings, enquiry forms, and an optional AI 
  chatbot for after-hours buyer questions. Custom domain supported on 
  paid plans.

- **Landlord portal** — Landlords update their own listings within rules 
  the agency sets. Enquiries stay with your negotiators.

- **Team management** — Assign staff to branches and listings. Control 
  who can publish, connect portals, manage billing, or use AI tools.

- **AI lead scoring** — Surface the leads most likely to convert so your 
  team prioritises the right calls every morning.

- **Website builder** — Build service and area pages with a block editor. 
  AI page drafting available on higher tiers.

- **Media manager** — Shared photo and file folders across branches.

- **Developments and schemes** — Group new-build plots so brochures, 
  spreadsheets, and portal feeds stay aligned.
